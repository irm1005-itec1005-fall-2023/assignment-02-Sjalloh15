Luxury Abayas

Abaya's are a type of loose - fitted dress that is mostly worn by muslim women around the world for modesty, they come in different sizes and different lengths. There are also many different colors you can choose from that fit you and your style.

Abaya Types

  • Elegant Abaya
  • Classic Abaya
  • Open/Closed Abaya
  • Batwing Abaya

Elegant Abaya

An elegant abaya is worn to events that require you to dress formally or for any special occasion, even though they are quite expense its worth it every single time and they also last a very long time.

They usually range from around $60 - $150

Classic Abaya

Classic abayas are ones you can wear on an everyday basis no matter the event. They're basically a casual outfit that comes in every color you can think of and is either plain or has a very simple design.

They usually range from about $40 - $60

Open/Closed Abaya

Open abayas and closed abayas can typically be the same thing depending on the design like for example you can have an open abaya with buttons that when buttoned up turn into a closed abaya, but there are also just closed abayas with no button or zipper and vice versa for open abayas meaning there is nothing to close them with.

Batwing Abaya

Batwing abayas are just like classic abayas but with bat like sleeves giving them a twist. They can also be made into an elegant abaya but they're mostly classic.This abaya style also comes in various colors and lengths.

They range from about the same price as the classic ones $40 - $60.
